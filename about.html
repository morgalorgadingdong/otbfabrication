Our Story

OTB Fabrication was born in Bend, OR when a Missouri Craftsman and a Kiwi Surfer happened to be introduced. From there, Will and Buzz combined their 10+ years of experience in woodworking, building and design to create their own Van and Custom Fabrication shop in July of 2021. Since then they’ve made custom builds from Sprinter Vans to Defenders to a converted Ambulance - and everything in between.

Buzz

Buzz is the Co-Founder of OTB Fabrication. Buzz is a Marine Electrician who specialises in building vintage wooden boats. He has a passion for woodworking and restoring old surfboards. Buzz was born and raised in New Zealand on his family Apple Orchard. Summer camping trips in their family caravan sparked his love of Van Life.

Will

Will is the Co-Founder of OTB Fabrication. Will is an Architect and Custom Wood Worker by trade and has been restoring vans since he was a teenager. Will grew up on a ranch in Missouri and credits his love of building to his Dad and God Father, who had him swinging a hammer when he was just 8 years old.
